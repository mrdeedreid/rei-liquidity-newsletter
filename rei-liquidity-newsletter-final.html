03 — Market Insight
What We’re Seeing This Month

The Austin duplex and fourplex market continues to show resilience despite elevated interest rates, with buyer activity picking up meaningfully in the 78745 and 78741 zip codes.

Investors with available capital are finding that off-market transactions are yielding more favorable pricing — particularly for owners who need certainty of close.

In the 5–25 unit apartment segment, cap rates have compressed modestly as buyer competition for stabilized assets intensifies.

Properties with strong occupancy histories and below-market rents are attracting the most activity.

“With cap rates stabilizing and rental demand holding firm, well-priced duplexes and small apartment buildings in established Austin neighborhoods are generating strong qualified interest within days of introduction.”
